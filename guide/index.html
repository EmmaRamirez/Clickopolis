Traits

Traits are assigned to your leader/Civilization at the start of the game, either manually or through the Personality Quiz. They carry an array of different bonuses throughout the game's progression. You gain one extra trait upon each restart, maxing out at 6. Likewise, opponent Civilizations also posses traits (2-4).
